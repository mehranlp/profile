Projects

Maximum Drawdown Dashboard
Maximum drawdown shows how much an investment drops from its peak before recovering. It’s a key metric for understanding potential losses and how long recovery might take. This project visualizes drawdown and wealth growth over time for assets like the S&P 500 and Bitcoin, using steady annual contributions. It’s especially helpful for comparing investment strategies, assessing risk tolerance, and evaluating long-term consistency across funds.
